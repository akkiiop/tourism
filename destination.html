1. Paris, France - The City of Love 💕

Why Visit?

Paris enchants visitors with its iconic Eiffel Tower, world-class art at the Louvre Museum, and romantic Seine River cruises. Known for its charming cafés, cobblestone streets, and exquisite pastries, Paris offers a timeless experience. Must-Do Activities: Take a sunset stroll along the Champs-Élysées. Marvel at the Gothic beauty of Notre-Dame Cathedral. Indulge in authentic croissants at a Parisian bakery.

Fun Fact:

Paris has over 1,800 bakeries!
